Stray Arrows - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Overview

Stray Arrows is a puzzle game. We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not collect personally identifiable information (name, email, phone, address). This policy explains what limited data the game and its advertising partner use to keep the game running.

Data Stored on Your Device

All game progress — level, coins, achievements, statistics, settings — is stored locally on your device via browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Stray Arrows displays advertisements through Google AdMob, a Google service. To deliver ads, AdMob may collect and process:

  • Device or other IDs — including the Advertising ID (AAID on Android, IDFA on iOS if permission is granted)
  • App interactions — events such as ad impressions, clicks, and completion of rewarded ads
  • Approximate location derived from IP address (no precise GPS)
  • Device information — model, OS version, language

This data is used for advertising, fraud prevention, and analytics. You can review and control this at any time:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset/Delete Advertising ID
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable for Stray Arrows

Full details: Google Advertising Policy and Google Privacy Policy.

Analytics

Stray Arrows itself does not run separate analytics. AdMob generates ad-serving metrics as described above.

Internet Connection

Gameplay (puzzle levels, progress tracking) works fully offline. An internet connection is used only to load advertisements.

Children's Privacy

Stray Arrows is rated for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 beyond what AdMob processes for ad serving (above). If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have information from a child that should not be retained, contact us.

Your Rights

You can reset your advertising identifier, opt out of personalized ads in your device settings, or uninstall the app at any time — all locally stored data will be removed on uninstall.

Changes

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
