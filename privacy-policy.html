Stray Arrows - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Overview

Stray Arrows is a puzzle game. We do not collect any personally identifiable information ourselves. The only data leaving your device comes from the third-party advertising SDK described below.

Data Stored on Your Device

All game progress (levels, coins, achievements, statistics, settings) is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is removed when you uninstall the app or use the in-app "Reset Progress" button.

Advertising — Google AdMob

Stray Arrows shows interstitial and rewarded ads provided by Google AdMob. To serve ads, AdMob may collect and process technical data from your device, including:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android) — only when you grant tracking permission
  • IP address and approximate location (country / region)
  • Device model, operating system version, and language
  • App-level interactions with ads (impressions, clicks)

If you decline the App Tracking Transparency prompt on iOS, or opt out of personalized ads on Android, AdMob will only show non-personalized (contextual) ads.

How Google uses this data is described in Google's Privacy & Terms and the AdMob & AdSense policies.

Internet Connection

Internet is only used to fetch ads and to deliver attribution events to Google. Gameplay itself works fully offline.

Children's Privacy

The game is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content. We do not knowingly request or collect personal information from children. AdMob is configured to comply with child-safety policies (no behavioral targeting of users under 13).

Changes

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
